Privacy Policy – Futures Scan

Last updated: 14 November 2025

1. General information

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Futures Scan (the “App”) processes user data. The App is an educational tool for analysing crypto futures markets and studying technical setups in real time. The App is for informational and training purposes only and does not provide investment services, does not execute trades, and does not allow users to deposit, hold, or withdraw any funds or cryptocurrencies.

3. What data we process

In the current test version of the Futures Scan App:

  • The App does not require creating an account and does not intentionally collect personal data such as name, e-mail address or phone number.
  • The App processes technical data necessary for it to function properly, in particular:
    • device information (e.g. model, operating system version),
    • application error and crash information (logs),
    • in-app settings stored locally on the device (e.g. selected pairs, filters, signal sensitivity).

This data may be processed solely for the purpose of maintaining the App, improving its stability, and analysing general usage patterns (without identifying a specific individual).

4. No accounts, funds or trading execution

The App does not create or manage user trading accounts, does not connect to any exchange or broker using API keys, and does not execute, modify, or cancel any orders. The App does not accept deposits, does not hold any fiat currency or cryptocurrencies for users, and does not provide any wallet, custodial, brokerage or exchange services.

All example signals, patterns and levels shown in the App are purely for educational and analytical purposes. Any real trading you decide to carry out must be done manually in your own exchange or broker account, outside of the App.

5. Data sources

The App receives data from:

  • the user – through settings and interactions with the interface, such as selection of pairs, trading style or filters;
  • external exchange services – in the form of market data (prices, volume, candles), which do not contain any personal data of the user.

6. Third-party services

The App may use services provided by Google or other entities, such as:

  • Google Play (distribution of the app),
  • Google Play Services (Android system services).

If additional services are used in the future (e.g. analytics, crash reporting), their names and functions will be added in updated versions of this Privacy Policy.

7. Purposes of processing

The technical data described above may be processed for the following purposes:

  • ensuring the proper operation of the App,
  • improving stability and performance,
  • detecting and fixing bugs,
  • analysing anonymous usage patterns (e.g. which features are used most often).

8. Legal basis for processing

If personal data is processed, the legal basis may include:

  • performance of a contract – processing is necessary to provide the App and its functions to the user;

  • user settings – until the App is uninstalled from the device or its data is cleared in system settings;
